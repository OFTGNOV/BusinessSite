About Us

My name is Tamai Richards, and I started this business from the ground up in the heart of Kingston, Jamaica. With a passion for fitness and a vision to create a community where everyone can achieve their health goals, I established WAIST MANAGEMENT. Our journey began with a small gym and has grown into a state-of-the-art facility, thanks to the dedication and support of our amazing clients and team.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission at Waist Management is to inspire a healthier community through engaging fitness programs, relatable humor, and a supportive environment. We aim to help members achieve their fitness goals while fostering connections and celebrating every milestone with laughter and positivity.

Our Vision Statement

To create a welcoming, lighthearted, and inclusive fitness space where humor meets health, empowering everyone to take charge of their wellness journey while having fun along the way.
